About the Bullyproof Campaign

The Bullyproof Campaign is dedicated to ending the bullying cycle in hockey. Our goal is to create a safe and inclusive environment for all players and coaches, regardless of their background or skill level.

We accomplish this through education, awareness, and support. We provide resources for players and coaches to learn about the impact of bullying, how to recognize it, and how to prevent it. We also provide support for those who have been affected by bullying, through counseling and mentorship programs.

Join us in our mission to make hockey a bully-free sport.
